urneighbour
aka Adam Price

Freelance illustrator , photographer, tattoo apprentice and multimedia visual art student at CMP Massey Wellington. Adam has been drawing since he could firmly grip a pencil and his style grows and changes as he does, though watery themes and gooey lines remain prevalent throughout the work. This style has now naturally transferred onto peoples skin with his recent aquisition of a tattoo machine and subsequent apprenticeship. His video work retains the themes of his other visual media, aquatic, flowing, vibrant and sparkly, and he has just begun working on the pilot episode for his directorial debut, The Willing Bodies.
